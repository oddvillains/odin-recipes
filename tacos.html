Tacos

Description

Perfect tacos just like from a street cart. I recommend using corn tortillas, but flour will work just as well. Add your own level of spicy salsa.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 lbs ground beef
  • 1 roma tomato, diced
  • 0.5 red onion, diced
  • 1 lime
  • 1 tbsp texmex spice
  • 1 cup black beans

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease.
  2. In same pan heat tortillas 5 seconds each side.
  3. Add ingredients to tortillas.
  4. Top with cheese, a squeeze of lime, or sour cream.
